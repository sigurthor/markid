The Foil Team Issue is the Aero Road bike of Team ORICA - GreenEDGE. Racing at the highest level on the World Tour, the Team races the Foil because it was developed to be faster and more efficient in all ROAD conditions. Ride this aero HMX carbon fiber frame with race ready components, and you'll definitely make the Team.

Frame: SCOTT FOIL HMX NET Carbon
Fork: Scott FOIL HMX NET Carbon steerer

Group set: Shimano Dura-Ace  
Brakes: Shimano Dura-Ace
Parts: Ritchey WCS Carbon
Wheels: SYNCROS RR1.5 carbon
